Related Sites

  • Model Organism Databases: FlyBase, Wormbase, and Saccharomyces Genome Database.
  • Databases for emerging model systems include DictyBase for Dictystelium, and the Tetrahymena Genome Database.
  • Signalling databases: STKE from Science, and the AfCS/Nature-affiliated Signaling Gateway.
  • Protein Kinase Resource, a general site about kinases and KinG another site containing genomic analyses of protein kinases.
  • Prediction of phosphorylation sites: NetPhos and Scansite, and a comprehensive database of mammalian protein phosphorylation sites at PhosphoSite.
  • Cell Signaling Technology has useful reference material and diagrams, including an interactive human kinome tree.
  • The Rice Kinome Database and PlantsP explore plant kinases and kinomes.
  • Domain Profile Searches by Pfam and SMART-HMMR.
  • For Macintosh bioinformatics resources: macbio.info
